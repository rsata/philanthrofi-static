How it works

Adpactor gives companies a way to involve the public in their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives. Through our local networks of publications and nonprofits, we let the public decide where to deploy resources and volunteer hours in the name of Corporate Philanthropy. Our Impact Advertisements engage the public in our common goal to promote CSR initiatives and increase donations to charitable organizations.

Each month, brands pledge volunteer hours, donations, and/or pro-bono work to the nonprofit community.

The Adpactor platform creates impact ads and distributes them to our network of publishers related to the brand.

At the end of the month, Adpactor tells the brands where to make their donations based on the responses from the impact ads.

Publisher Benefits:

  • Consistant source of funding to monitize your site
  • Professional ads with fast load times to keep your site running quickly.
  • Quick implementation - Just copy and paste our code snippet into your site. We handle the rest so no need to track down advertisers for the content.
  • Know that your site is being used to support local charities.

Brands

  • Automated matching algorithm to pair your business with the right charities and publications. For example, an outdoor clothing company's ads get pushed to nature blogs to support conservation and outdoor education charities.
  • Hand picked and vetted nonprofit network.
  • Local charities.

Nonprofits

  • Increased visibility and marketing.
  • Access to partner network.
  • Increased donations.
