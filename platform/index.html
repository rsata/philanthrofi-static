How it works

Adpactor gives companies a way to involve the public in their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives. Through our local networks of publications and non profits, we let the public decide where to deploy resources and volunteer hours in the name of Corporate Philanthropy. Our Impact Advertisements engage the public in our common goal to promote CSR initiatives and increase donations to charitable organizations.

Publisher Benefits:

  • Consistant source of funding to monitize your site
  • Professional ads with fast load times to keep your site running quickly.
  • Quick implementation - Just copy and paste our code snippet into your site. We handle the rest so no need to track down advertisers for the content.
  • Know that your site is being used to support local charities.

Brands

  • Automated matching algorithm to pair your business with the right charities and publications. For example, an outdoor clothing company's ads get pushed to nature blogs to support conservation and outdoor education charities.
  • Hand picked and vetted non profit network.
  • Local charities.

Non Profits

  • Increased visibility and marketing.
  • Access to partner network.
  • Increased donations.
